About Me


I'm a Front-end Developer/Web Designer with around 7 years of experience. Some of my skills are HTML5, CSS/CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Less, Sass, Git, AJAX, PHP, SQL, ReactJS, Redux, Html Emails, Litmus, SVG Animations, Wordpress, Gulp, Grunt, NPM, Yarn, Photoshop and Illustrator

I love to learn and improve both my programming and web design skills. Even though I've learned a lot through all these years by doing courses, practices, working experiences, I know that there's a lot to learn still, so I'm willing and open to learn from others and do my best as a professional, and this is because the most important thing about what I do, is that I love my profession as a Front end Developer.

I originally graduated as a Graphic Designer back in 2011. My final project, as a graphic designer student, was designing and coding a Website, so since then I fell in love with web development. In addition to that, I finished two Udacity Nanodegree programs, one was the Frontend Development Nanodegree and the other was the React Nanodegree program.
